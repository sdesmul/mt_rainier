MT Rainier Cattery

MT Rainier is a small cattery in Auburn, Washington that specializes in Sphinx, Domestic Shorthairs, and Domestic Longhairs.

Every kitten is raised as a part of our family. Every kitten comes with their first two sets of shots.

Kittens are TICA registered. Every King and Queen pass an HCM scan before being bred to ensure healthy kittens.
